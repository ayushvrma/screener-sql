Welcome to Screener NL

Turn plain English into a screener.in query. Two engines, in this order:

1) Rule-based translator — always on

Zero setup, ~1 ms, works for the vast majority of common screening ideas (P/E, ROE, D/E, growth CAGRs, ownership, scores, moving averages, etc.).

2) LLM fallback — bring your own key (BYOK)

When your English uses phrasings the rule engine doesn't recognise, the panel can call an LLM to translate. Pick any provider — the key is stored locally in chrome.storage.local and only ever sent to the provider you selected.

Don't have one? Get one from the provider →

3) Try it

Open screener.in/screen/raw/ and look for the panel bottom-right. Or click the extension icon anywhere on the web.

Tip: ⌘/Ctrl + Enter in the panel input runs the translation.
